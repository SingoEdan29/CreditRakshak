Privacy Policy

Credit Rakshak

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

Credit Rakshak ("we", "our", or "us") is developed and operated by SingoEdan. We are committed to protecting your personal information and complying with applicable data protection regulations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, transmit, and protect your data when you use the Credit Rakshak mobile application.

1. Overview

Credit Rakshak provides users in India with tools to estimate and evaluate their credit score based on personal, financial, and device-related data. Our goal is to provide accurate credit assessments while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information directly provided by you, including but not limited to:

  • Full name and contact information (phone number, email)
  • Identification documents such as PAN card photos
  • Permanent city or location information
  • Employment, income, and KYC-related information

Device and Usage Information

We may automatically collect information about your device and app usage to provide better service, including:

  • Device identifiers, model, and operating system version
  • Network operator and connectivity data
  • App usage logs and analytics for service improvement

3. KYC and Financial Information

To generate accurate credit assessments, we may collect additional information as part of the Know Your Customer (KYC) process, such as:

  • Residence type
  • Employment type and job level
  • Education level
  • Expected loan amount
  • Loan purpose
  • Marital status
  • Monthly income range
  • Number of dependents
  • Payment method and emergency contact relationship
  • Religious belief (optional)
  • Employment status and years of employment

4. App Permissions

Location

Location helps us verify city information and detect abnormal login activity for security purposes. Location data is encrypted and used exclusively for risk assessment and security.

Camera & Photo Gallery

Used to capture or upload identification documents for verification. Images are encrypted during transmission and storage, and will not be shared with third parties.

Device Phone Status

Information such as device identifier and phone status helps prevent fraud and ensures accurate identity verification.

5. Data Transmission and Encryption

Collected data may be transmitted to our secure server at https://crsk29.com. All sensitive data is encrypted using AES encryption to ensure confidentiality and prevent unauthorized access.

6. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Identity verification
  • Credit score calculation and financial risk assessment
  • Fraud detection and account security
  • Service improvement and analytics
  • Compliance with legal obligations

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may be shared only when required for service operation or as mandated by law. Third-party service providers may process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or meet legal requirements. Once data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. You may also withdraw consent for certain data processing activities. For assistance, please contact us at multimediakaneta@gmail.com.
